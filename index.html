Got questions!!

How do I create a playlist!!

Playlists are a great way to save collecions of music, either for your own listening or to share.

To create one:

1. Tap Your Library.
2. Tap CREATE.
3. Give your playlist a name.
4. Start adding songs (and we'll help you along).

How do I activate Data Saver mode!!

1. Tap Home.
2. Tap Settings.
3. tap Data Saver
4. Switch on Data Saver.

Is it only possible to shuffle play music!!

Any playlist with the shuffle icon will play on shuffle.

Some playlists won't have the shuffle icon, so you can tap to play it.

Where can I find Podcasts!!

Tap Search. Under Browse All, tsp Podcasts.
